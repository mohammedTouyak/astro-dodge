# ASTRO DODGE – Game Design Document

## Concept

Astro Dodge is a fast-paced arcade survival game.

The player controls a spaceship navigating through asteroid fields.

The objective is to survive as long as possible while increasing the score.

---

## Gameplay Loop

Player moves spaceship  
↓  
Asteroids spawn  
↓  
Player dodges asteroids  
↓  
Score increases over time  
↓  
Collision → Game Over

---

## Player Mechanics

- Horizontal movement
- Screen boundary limits
- Collision detection

---

## Enemy Mechanics

Asteroids:

- spawn from top of screen
- move downward
- destroy player on collision

---

## Scoring

Score increases based on survival time.

---

## Difficulty

Difficulty increases over time by:

- faster asteroids
- higher spawn rate

---

## Leaderboard

Top scores are saved locally and displayed in a leaderboard.
